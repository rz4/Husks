## Why These Are Deferred

**locke → core, designs.transport**
- Locke is the surface language parser
- Defers heavy parsing dependencies until actually parsing .locke files
- Only needed when running `husks run` with .locke syntax

**designs.ir → build, build.site, locke**  
- IR is the intermediate representation layer
- Defers the entire build engine until actually compiling designs
- Only needed when converting surface → IR → execution

**setup → cli.surface, resources, utils.console**
- Setup is the package init/selftest module
- Defers CLI rendering until running `husks init` or `husks doctor`
- Avoids loading UI dependencies on simple imports

**cli.cmd.validate → setup**
- Internal helper `_resolve_conformance` for finding test vectors
- Only used in specific doctor subcommands
- Could be hoisted but left as internal optimization

## Key Insight

All 9 violations are **same-layer or downward** deferred imports:
- No upward imports (would violate DAG)
- No cycles (all were eliminated in phases 0-2)
- All are performance optimizations to defer expensive subsystems
